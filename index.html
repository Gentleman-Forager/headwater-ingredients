What’s Inside Headwater

Ingredients & Science

Explore the real, natural ingredients behind Headwater's clean energy and wellness support.

Back to Product Page
Carbonated Water

Pure, filtered water infused with gentle carbonation for clean hydration and refreshing mouthfeel without heavy sweetness or syrupy texture.

All-Natural Agave Syrup

A low-glycemic sweetener used in small amounts to deliver light sweetness without spikes or crashes. Supports flavor balance and overall taste experience.

Citric Acid

A fruit-derived acidifier used to brighten flavor and support shelf stability. Chosen for its ability to provide tartness without harsh acidity.

L-Theanine

An amino acid found in green tea that supports calm alertness, mental clarity, and focus—without jitters or overstimulation.

Vitamin C

An antioxidant that helps support healthy immune function and cellular protection. Included to optimize immune wellness support.

Lion’s Mane Extract (Fruiting Body)

Certified-organic Lion’s Mane extract, made only from the fruiting body. Supports cognitive clarity, memory, and focus—sourced for purity, potency, and consistency.

Chaga Extract (Fruiting Body)

Sustainably harvested Chaga extract from wild birch trees. Rich in polyphenols and antioxidants that help defend cells from stress and support overall resilience.

Natural Flavor System (Blueberry / Tangerine)

Natural flavors crafted to highlight the ingredients—not mask them. Chosen to create a clean, bright taste experience with no mushroom flavor or aftertaste.
